FisTX is a platform of research-based fisheries technology. We are here as an oasis of fisheries and giving holistic solutions for fishery. FisTx presents as a friend and partner of the fish and shrimp cultivators in Indonesia with a multifunctional application. FisTx also responds to climate changes by shrimp and fish urban farming so that it can be efficient for transportation costs and reduces carbon footprint.
